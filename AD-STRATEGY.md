# Meta Ads Strategy - Government Grants Discovery

## Campaign Overview
- **Goal:** Validate demand for grants discovery + collect founder data
- **Budget:** ₹25,000 ($300) for 1 week
- **Duration:** Feb 19 - Feb 25, 2026
- **Target audience:** Indian startup founders
- **Success metric:** 500-1000 form completions at ₹25-50 per completion

---

## Ad Creative (3 Variations)

### Ad 1: Time Savings Angle
**Headline:** "Stop wasting 20+ hours hunting government grants"
**Body:** "We found all 200+ central and state grants. Answer 5 quick questions and see which ones you actually qualify for in 5 minutes."
**CTA:** "Find My Grants"

### Ad 2: Opportunity Angle
**Headline:** "Missing ₹50+ lakh in government grants for your startup?"
**Body:** "Indian startups miss 70% of relevant grants because they're scattered across 200+ programs. We matched your profile to every grant you qualify for."
**CTA:** "See My Grants"

### Ad 3: Social Proof Angle
**Headline:** "500+ Indian startup founders already matched with their grants"
**Body:** "Don't leave money on the table. Get a personalized list of government grants + application guidance in 5 minutes."
**CTA:** "Join Now"

## Key Metrics to Track

### Daily Monitoring
- **Click-through rate (CTR):** Target 3%+
- **Cost per click:** Target ₹50 or lower
- **Form completion rate:** Target 80%+ of clicks
- **Cost per submission:** Target ₹25-50

### Weekly Analysis
- **Total impressions:** 50,000-100,000
- **Total clicks:** 1,500-3,000
- **Total form submissions:** 500-1,000
- **Founder breakdown:**
  - By sector: Which sectors most interested?
  - By stage: Pre-seed vs Seed vs Series A distribution
  - By location: Geographic spread
  - By intent: Which grants got marked as interested?

---

## What We Learn from Ads

1. **Does the problem exist?** (High CTR = people care)
2. **Who's most interested?** (Sector/stage/location breakdown)
3. **Which grants matter most?** (Click patterns on results)
4. **Pricing sensitivity?** (Track intent signals)
5. **Tone/messaging that resonates?** (Best performing ad)

## Next Steps After Campaign

### If Successful (500+ submissions)
1. Analyze founder segments
2. Build database for top 3 segments first
3. Launch MVP with pro tier at ₹5000/month
4. Email all 500 submissions with free access offer
5. Target: 20-30 paying customers

### If Less Successful (200-500 submissions)
1. Iterate ads for 1 more week
2. Test different creative angles
3. Expand audience targeting
4. Consider pivoting offer

### If Failed (<200 submissions)
1. May indicate lack of market demand
2. Consider pivoting to different angle
3. Or test different target audience
